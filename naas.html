Al-Naas

آل ناس

بِسمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحمٰنِ الرَّحيمِ قُل أَعوذُ بِرَبِّ النّاسِ

Qul a'uzu birabbin naas

Say: I seek refuge with the Lord and Cherisher of Mankind,

مَلِكِ النّاسِ

Malikin naas

The King of Mankind,

إِلٰهِ النّاسِ

Ilaahin naas

The Judge of Mankind,

مِن شَرِّ الوَسواسِ الخَنّاسِ

Min sharril was waasil khannaas

From the mischief of the Whisperer, who withdraws.

مالَّذى يُوَسوِسُ فى صُدورِ النّاسِ

Al lazee yuwas wisu fee sudoorin naas

Who whispers into the hearts of Mankind,

مِنَ الجِنَّةِ وَالنّاسِ

Minal jinnati wan naas

Among Jinns and among men.
