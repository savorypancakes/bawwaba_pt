Salah is a great website to learn how to pray salah! Whether you're an adult wanting to learn, or maybe you're looking for a way to teach your children namaz or even just someone wanting to brush up on how you should be praying - Salah is the website for you!

For those who doesn't know what salah is. Salah is a type of prayer that is performed by muslims in a daily basis as a religious obligation towards the Islamic faith. To perform Salah, you will first need to wait for the right time, be in a clean place and be clean. In addition you will need to perform this 5 times a day.
