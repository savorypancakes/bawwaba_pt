قريش

ألِإِيلَٰفِ قُرَيْشٍ

Li-ilaafi quraish

For the covenants of security and safeguard enjoyed by the Quraish,

أإِۦلَٰفِهِمْ رِحْلَةَ ٱلشِّتَآءِ وَٱلصَّيْفِ

Elaafihim rihlatash shitaa-i wass saif

Their covenants covering journeys by winter and summer,

فَلْيَعْبُدُوا۟ رَبَّ هَٰذَا ٱلْبَيْتِ

Fal y'abudu rabba haazal-bait

Let them adore the Lord of this House,

ٱلَّذِىٓ أَطْعَمَهُم مِّن جُوعٍ وَءَامَنَهُم مِّنْ خَوْفٍۭ

Allazi at'amahum min ju'inw-wa-aamana hum min khawf

Who provides them with food against hunger, and with security against fear.
