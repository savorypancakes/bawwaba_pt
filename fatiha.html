Al-Fatiha

الفاتحة

بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Bismillah Rahmani Rahime.

In The Name of God, The Beneficent, The Merciful.

الْحَمْدُ للّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ

Alhamdu lillaahi Rabbil 'aalameen.

All praise is only God's, the Lord of the Worlds.

الرَّحْمـنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Ar-Rahmaanir-Raheem.

The Beneficent, The Merciful.

مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ

Maaliki Yawmid-Deen.

Master of the Day of Judgement.

إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

Iyyaaka na'budu wa lyyaaka nasta'een.

Thee do we worship and of Thee (only) do we seek help.

اهدِنَــــا الصِّرَاطَ المُستَقِيمَ

Ihdinas-Siraatal-Mustaqeem.

Guide us on the Straight Path.

صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنعَمتَ عَلَيهِمْ غَيرِ المَغضُوبِ عَلَيهِمْ وَلاَ الضَّالِّينَ

Siraatal-lazeena an'amta 'alaihim ghayril-maghdoobi 'alaihim wa lad-daaalleen.

The path of those upon whom Thou hast bestowed Thy bounties, not of those inflicted with Thy wrath, nor gone astray.
