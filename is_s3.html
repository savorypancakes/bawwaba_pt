Step 2

Remain in standing position which you need to hold your left arm with your right arm while overlapping them and recite the opening prayer of the Qur'an the Al-Fatiha:

  1. Bismillahi Rahmani Rahime

  2. Alhamdou lillahi rabil alamine

  3. A rahmani Rahime

  4. Maliki yawmiddine

  5. Iyaka na boodoo wa iyaka nasta'ine

  6. Ihdina siratal mostakime Sirital ladina an'amta alayhime makdoobi alayhime wa lak da line.

    7.

To be followed by any complete surah from the quran either long or short. For example Al Ikhlas:

Bismillahi Rahmani Rahime

  2. Allah hus-samad

  3. Lam yalid wa lam yoolad
