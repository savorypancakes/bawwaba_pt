An-Nasr

قريش

إإِذَا جَآءَ نَصْرُ ٱللَّهِ وَٱلْفَتْحُ

Iza jaa-a nas rullahi walfath

When comes the Help of God, and Victory,

وَرَأَيْتَ ٱلنَّاسَ يَدْخُلُونَ فِى دِينِ ٱللَّهِ أَفْوَاجًا

Wa ra-aitan naasa yadkhuloona fee deenil laahi afwajah

And thou dost see the people enter God's Religion in crowds,

فَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ وَٱسْتَغْفِرْهُ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ تَوَّابًۢا

Fa sab bih bihamdi rabbika was taghfir, innahu kaana tawwaaba

Celebrate the praises of thy Lord, and pray for His Forgiveness: For He is Oft-Returning in Grace and Mercy
